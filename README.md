## About Me

I am currently a Computer Science student at **Assumption University** of Thailand. I love building software and writing about technology. 

Right now, my main focus is **Machine Learning and Data Analytics**. I am working on various AI and Machine Learning projects to understand how they work in the real world. To keep my coding skills sharp, I also do **UI/UX design and frontend web developement** using mostly React and Figma.

I am also learning:
- **App Development** and **Computer Vision** to expand my skillset.
- Putting more effort into **UX programs** because I enjoy making things look good and easy to use.

I am always open to chatting about tech or helping out with things I know. Feel free to reach out!
